Lyon ties a Sabres record with his ninth straight win - Audacy

Source:Audacy.com
Author:Paul Hamilton

On Saturday Alex Lyon won his ninth straight game which tied a Buffalo Sabres record held for almost 50 years. Gerry Desjardins did it in 1976-77. Lyon did it in style posting a 26 save shutout in a 5-0 victory over the New York Islanders.
