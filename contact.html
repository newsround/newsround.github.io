Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe to your newsletter?
You can subscribe to our newsletter by entering your email address in the newsletter section at the bottom of any page on our website. You can also manage your subscription preferences through the link provided in every newsletter email.
Can I advertise on Newsround?
Yes, we offer advertising opportunities for businesses. Please contact our advertising team at ads@newsround.com for rates and available advertising options.
How do I report inaccurate news?
We take accuracy seriously. If you find inaccurate information, please email us at accuracy@newsround.com with the article link and details about the inaccuracy. Our editorial team will investigate promptly.
Do you have a mobile app?
Currently, we offer a mobile-optimized website that works great on all devices. We're developing native mobile apps for iOS and Android, which will be released later this year.
How can I contribute content?
We welcome guest contributions from qualified journalists and subject matter experts. Please send your pitch along with writing samples to editorial@newsround.com. Our editorial team will review your submission.
Is Newsround available internationally?
Yes, Newsround is available worldwide. We provide news coverage from over 50 countries and our website is accessible globally. Some features may vary by region based on local regulations.
